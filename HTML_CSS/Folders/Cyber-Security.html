Cyber Security: Analysis of the digital world

EdYoda | 23 Jul 2019

When computers were standalone devices, security was a minimal concern. Mainframes had their own protected facilities. Slowly and steadily as computing became more personal, security became the personal responsibility too. The use of computers increasingly involves networks of computers for messaging, on-line transactions, remote hosting. It started including the opportunities for the attack which have multiplied exponentially. Arriving at the security part became a major concern to drive out businesses well and generate revenues without leading to the losses due to the breach of security

Cybersecurity has become a major concern of businesses and governments. As the data saved of clouds and other means has the exposure us to hackers, business competitors and other predators with malicious intent. Cyber breaches have led to many damages including, public property and loss of life. Data breach affects large MNCs, small and medium enterprises in a very harmful way. It would lead access to confidential information, accounts which can be viewed, copied or used by anyone with harmful intention.

Enterprise Cybersecurity
The process of protecting the business’s on-premise and cloud-based infrastructure as well as evaluate third-party providers and secure the growing number of endpoints connected to the system via the Internet. The web is an ever-growing platform because it is connected to the range of multiple devices from smartphones to sensor-equipped manufacturing robots that are connected via the internet.

According to news, malicious cyber-attacks and not strict security protocols led to massive breaches of personal information in 2018 and the largest breach was in India. In India, the government ID database Aadhaar reported suffered multiple breaches that compromised the records of 1.1 billion registered citizens.

It was also reported in January that was selling access to the database at a rate of Rs 500 for 10 minutes. Another data breach was by the famous application MyFitnessPal application and around 50 million Facebook users are affected. Thus, it is important for any company having a strong Enterprise cybersecurity architecture.

Significance of Enterprise Cybersecurity
You must have the news of a major data breach or may have heard about the data leaks. Those are just reported breaches that harm a famous or big organization. The internet is poised to connect more than 50 billion devices by 2020, yet there’s no business looking a way to overcome data breaches despite the growing risk. They are only focusing on growing data and its analysis.

Cyber-attacks are lucrative:Cybercriminals understand the importance of the data organizations. They seek some type of benefit and will invest in various techniques, tools, and technology to achieve their malicious intent.

Cost of data breaches are high:When a cybersecurity breach occurs, the consequences of the incidents are costly and devastating to the organization. Therefore, every business organization needs a robust enterprise cybersecurity architecture to protect confidential information. Nowadays, it is also important to maintain network security because everything is connected to the web platform.

Network Security
Network Security is the practices and technologies that a business’s uses to protect its IT infrastructure. IT infrastructure is made up of all of the data, programs, applications, web networks, software, and hardware utilized and managed by businesses.

Effective management of your IT systems is important on two fronts: First, it ensures any digital assets that are client or public-facing – like your website, email accounts or a payment page which makes the IT structure safe, reliable, compliant and safe to use. Second, it also protects your own protection so that data and information utilized in your business operations get used correctly.

Some networks security measures for every business include data protection activities like:

Password- protected computer and mobile devices

Firewalls or similar internal network controls

Anti-virus, anti-spyware and anti-malware software

Protected cloud-based storage and local device storage

Significance of Network Security

As public pressure mounts, issues like data privacy and personal data breaches arise. Proper network security means you can deliver your goods and services to your customers timely. The digital world is exposed at a higher level as more and more people expect everyday services, transactions, and information to be available at the fingertips. Therefore, it is important to have a secured connected architecture which will lead to the security of your confidential information.

Conclusion

A business suffers in many ways when its data is breached, such as compensating affected customers, having to pay for the right protection architecture and keeping it updated all times. Data breaches occur when an outside organization or an individual gains access to your digital connection and sensitive information. As the impact of data breach leads to the leak of personal or financial details, those people are risked to identity theft. Data breaches draw a big million- dollar hole in the world’s financial pocket.

Implementing a successful cyber-defense program against real-world attacks is important for every organization. It leads to mitigating and minimizing the effects of cyberattacks. Building a strong Enterprise cybersecurity system is a solid foundation upon which any organization can grow hesitantly.

Nowadays, many organizations are hiring cybersecurity experts to help them build a strong enterprise cybersecurity system. Therefore, Enterprise cybersecurity is essential to learn for those want to build their future in digital technologies.
